Media refers in general to the various channels of communication, mass communication, more so. The various channels such as television, radio, newspaper and now the internet are popular media besides magazines and journals. The earliest form of media that we all know is the introduction of newspapers and books. Over time, various new means have been developed such as the televisions, the radios and the emergence of the internet. Now, a lot of people cannot do without them in their lives. The role this plays is creating a bond and strengthening society. The media is the means of spreading information swiftly and through a wide coverage area and this helps with the creation of awareness.
